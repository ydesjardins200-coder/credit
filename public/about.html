About iBoost

Built by people who've seen the industry from the inside.

Twenty-plus years in North American finance told us the same story over and over: good people, trapped by credit they didn't understand. iBoost is our answer.

Why iBoost exists

The same story, over and over.

Across mortgages, personal loans, and car financing, we kept watching the same pattern: capable North Americans — people earning well, living responsibly — getting locked out of opportunities or charged more than they should be, simply because nobody had shown them how credit actually works.

iBoost exists to fix that. Not with gimmicks or score-juicing tricks, but by doing the two things that genuinely move the needle: reporting on-time payments to the bureaus, and teaching the fundamentals in plain English. Build real credit. Keep it. Use it when it matters.

What we believe

Long-term financial health, not short-term score hacks.

A credit score that climbs fast on tricks also falls fast. The score that actually changes your life is the one you build on habits, understanding, and consistency — and that's the one we help you build.

Important to know

What iBoost is not.

The credit-building category has a lot of noise. Here's where we stand.

We are not a lender.

iBoost doesn't issue loans or extend credit. We help you build the credit profile lenders look for — so when the time comes, you get better terms from the real thing.

We don't sell your data.

Your financial information stays yours. We use it to serve you — reporting, analysis, guidance — and that's it. No data brokers. Full stop.

We don't promise specific score increases.

Anyone who does is lying. Your results depend on your starting point and your consistency. What we promise is the system that actually works, every time, when you use it.

We don't hide fees or lock you into contracts.

Free plan available, no contract, cancel in two clicks. If iBoost isn't working for you, you leave. That's the deal.

Who we are

A small team with a long history.

We're a team of 20, based in Quebec, Canada — growing quickly as more people find us. We've spent our careers inside the financial industry: underwriting, lending, risk, fintech. iBoost is what we've always wanted these products to be.
