Bureau reporting

Reported to every major bureau, every month.

Most credit-builder products report to just one bureau. That means lenders using the other bureaus never see your progress. iBoost reports to every major bureau in both countries — so the work you do always counts.

  • 3 U.S. bureaus — Equifax, TransUnion, Experian.
  • 2 Canadian bureaus — Equifax Canada and TransUnion Canada.
  • Same day cycle — your payment is reported to every bureau at the same monthly point.
  • Visible within 30 days — the new tradeline shows up on your report after the first payment.

Credit mechanics

What actually moves your score.

Your FICO score is a recipe with four ingredients. iBoost is designed to help you move the two biggest ones — the ones worth 65% of the formula.

  • Payment history (35%) is the single biggest factor. Every on-time iBoost payment feeds directly into it.
  • Utilization (30%) is how much of your available credit you're using. A bigger reported tradeline lowers it automatically.
  • Credit age (15%) grows on its own once your tradeline is on file — the longer you stay, the better.
  • Credit mix & new inquiries (20%) matter less, and we don't trigger hard inquiries to protect that score factor.
