Overview

iBoost ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our credit-building service and related tools.

Information we collect

To provide our service, we collect personal information including your name, email address, date of birth, Social Insurance Number (Canada) or Social Security Number (U.S.), address, and financial account details. We also collect usage data about how you interact with our platform.

How we use your information

We use your information to operate our credit-building service, report your payments to credit bureaus (Equifax, TransUnion, and Experian where applicable), provide personalized AI-powered advice, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

Who we share it with

We share limited information with credit bureaus for reporting purposes, payment processors for billing, and service providers that help us operate the platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. To exercise these rights, email privacy@iboost.com.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact our privacy team at privacy@iboost.com.
